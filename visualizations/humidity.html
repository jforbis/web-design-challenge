Latitude vs. Humidity

Latitude vs Humidity Explanation:

  • This scatter plot is comparing humidity across a range of latitudes.
  • It appears that higher humidity is achieved fairly constantly from -40 degrees to 70 degrees latitude.
  • Given the random list of cities, there is a large cluster of cities between 40 and 70 degrees latitude with a very high humidity percentage (roughly 80-95%)
